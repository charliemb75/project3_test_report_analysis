# UNEVEN TIRE WEAR (OUTSIDE)\n\n

## GENERAL DATA\n\n
- **Models affected:** M02\n\n
- **Parts affected:** P0030\n\n
- **Last modification:** 20/05/2026\n\n

## DESCRIPTION OF THE PROBLEM\n\n
The tire shows severe uneven wear, with the center tread much more worn and smoothed than the shoulders. The tread depth appears significantly reduced in the middle section, indicating abnormal wear rather than uniform aging.\n\n

### Possible causes:\n
- Overinflation causing excessive center tread wear
- Prolonged driving with incorrect tire pressure
- Tire misalignment contributing to uneven wear
- Suspension or steering issues affecting contact patch
- Tire overloading or poor load distribution\n\n

### Possible consequences:\n
- Reduced traction, especially in wet conditions
- Increased risk of hydroplaning
- Longer braking distances
- Higher chance of tire failure or blowout if wear becomes excessive
- Poor handling and reduced vehicle stability\n\n

### Possible measures to prevent it:
- Maintain correct tire inflation pressure according to manufacturer specifications
- Perform regular wheel alignment checks
- Rotate tires at recommended intervals
- Inspect suspension and steering components for wear or damage
- Design tires and recommend fabrication tolerances that promote more even load distribution
